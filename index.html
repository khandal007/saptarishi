Discovery Method

RV
Radial Velocity/Doppler wobble

It is an indirect method for detecting exoplanets which depends on measuring the small reflex motion of a star caused by an exoplanet companion as the two orbit their common center of gravity.

Transit
Transit Photometry

This method detects distant planets by measuring the minute dimming of a star as an orbiting planet passes between it and the Earth. The passage of a planet between a star and the Earth is called a "transit."

Microlensing
Gravitational microlensing

It is an astronomical phenomenon due to the gravitational lens effect. It can be used to detect objects that range from the mass of a planet to the mass of a star, regardless of the light they emit.This is capable of finding the furthest planet.
